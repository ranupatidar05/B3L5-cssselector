Heavy rain batters parts of Gujarat; IMD issues red alert for next 2 days

Gujarat rains: Heavy showers have battered the parts of the state causing floods in low-lying areas of Saurashtra and Kutch.

An aerial view of the flooded Sutrapada city after heavy rainfall, in Gir Somnath on Thursday.(ANI)

The India Meteorological Department (IMD) on Friday issued a 'red' alert for Gujarat for Saturday and Sunday while noting that the state is likely to get "heavy to very heavy rainfall (more than 204.4 mm)" on these days.
